    Services and Treatments

    Brief description

    Cost

    Complete Medical Assessment
    Whether it be your pet's first visit at our health clinic or one of its many, we will do a routine checkup where we examined several aspects of your pet, such as their weight, temperature, teeth, fur and/or skin, and stool samples. We will also provide any required vitamins and/or supplements and will discuss the animal's vaccination history.
    $75

    Flea Control
    If your pet is experiencing any flea irritation, we will try our best to amend the issue without the removal of any coats. If our deep cleaning techniques do not do the trick, then we will explore other options with the owner.
    $30

    Dental
    Your pet will receive a dental cleaning at our health clinic, along with a checkup to catch any potential gum disease, cancer, or other harmful conditions.
    $25

    Tonometry
    Tonometry is the specialization of eye care. If your pet has any visual difference to their eyes or seems to be experiencing discomfort, we will do our best to provide a prognosis and solution.
    $50

    Dermatology
    Dermatology is the specialization of skin care. Our health clinic has an in-house specialist who produces several ointments and solutions to aid with any medical issues your pet may be experiencing.
    $50
